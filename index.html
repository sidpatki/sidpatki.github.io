About

I am a Senior Research Scientist at General Motors, working on Robotics and AI. Previously, I was a Research Scientist at Meta and an Applied Scientist at Amazon. I received my PhD in Electrical Engineering from the University of Rochester in 2023, advised by Prof. Thomas Howard, where my research focused on language understanding and perception for robots operating in dynamic environments. Outside of research, I enjoy watercolor painting and playing Badminton.
